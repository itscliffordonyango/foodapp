Madishipoa App

Project thesis

According to a study conducted by Stanford University, 80% of people today are unaware of the ingredients in the food they consume daily. Additionally, many individuals rarely read the nutritional information or ingredient lists on canned and packaged foods—they typically glance at the label and price, then make a purchase.

Madishipoa App aims to change this norm by promoting food awareness and healthier living. It is a multipurpose application designed to educate users about the nutritional value of various foods, offer recipe suggestions, and help users make the most of the ingredients they already have at home. Whether you're a beginner in the kitchen or a seasoned cook, Madishipoa brings knowledge and creativity to your fingertips.

Amazing Features

Madishipoa is not just an app—it's your kitchen companion. Here's what it offers:

  • Food Ingredient Education: Learn about the food you eat—its origin, health benefits, and risks if consumed in excess.
  • Nutritional Awareness: Get comprehensive nutritional breakdowns of everyday meals and products.
  • Interactive Recipes: Access a vast library of easy-to-follow recipes for all occasions.
  • Scan My Fridge: Use your phone's camera to scan the items in your fridge, and get smart suggestions from our AI chefs on what to cook.
  • Diet Tracking & Recommendations: Stay healthy with custom meal plans and dietary recommendations based on your preferences.

Brand Identity

Madishipoa embodies a modern, health-conscious lifestyle. Our brand stands for transparency, convenience, and empowerment—helping users make informed food choices without sacrificing taste or time. We believe knowledge is power, especially when it comes to what you eat.

Graphics Design

Our visual identity is built on a clean, vibrant, and minimalistic design that reflects freshness and clarity. From the icons to the user interface, every detail has been designed to give users an intuitive and delightful experience while navigating the app.

Videography

Our promotional videos and in-app cooking tutorials are crafted with professional-grade videography. These short videos not only teach users how to prepare meals but also explain the nutritional benefits of each ingredient used—bringing education and entertainment together in your kitchen.

UI/UX Design

Madishipoa is user-friendly and responsive. Whether you’re scanning ingredients, following a recipe, or exploring new meal ideas, our intuitive UI/UX ensures a smooth experience from start to finish. The app is optimized for both novice and experienced users.

Application

Built using cutting-edge technologies, Madishipoa is available for Android and iOS platforms. It runs smoothly on most devices and uses minimal data to ensure accessibility for users in areas with limited connectivity.

SEO/Online Marketing

Our SEO and digital marketing strategies are focused on reaching users who are looking for smart ways to eat better, cook creatively, and live healthier. Through blog posts, social media engagement, influencer collaborations, and app store optimization, we aim to create a strong digital footprint that attracts and retains users globally.
